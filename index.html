About Us

At FRESHONICA, we believe that every moment deserves a fresh and vibrant experience. Our passion is to craft premium, non-alcoholic bar syrups that elevate the ordinary into extraordinary. Whether you're a mixologist at home or a professional barista, FRESHONICA brings the freshness of real fruits and unique flavours straight to your glass.
